# Code of Conduct

This repository is a public-facing product front maintained by a small team at RCS (Almaty, Kazakhstan). We want issues and discussions to be a useful signal for the next release, not a punishment for engaging with us.

## Expectations

- **Be specific.** A reproducer beats a rant. If it helps, assume we're on your side and skip the framing.
- **Be honest about scope.** Say what you tried, what happened, what you expected. Half the fix is usually in the problem statement.
- **Be patient.** Our SLA is 1 business day for response on `support@rcs.kz`, 24 hours on `security@rcs.kz`. We'll hit that; we cannot always hit faster.
- **Assume competence.** Both directions. We will not patronise you; we ask you to extend the same.

## Not welcome

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination on any axis.
- Off-topic political or religious advocacy in issue threads.
- Disclosure of security findings via public issues — see [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
- Attempts to pressure us into bypassing our license verification or publishing source we've chosen not to publish. You're welcome to not use our product; you're not welcome to waste our maintainers' time debating it.

## Enforcement

Repeated violations will result in issue locks or a ban from the repository. Serious violations (harassment, doxxing, coordinated brigading) will result in an immediate ban and, where warranted, a referral to GitHub Trust & Safety.

Report incidents to `support@rcs.kz` with `[CONDUCT]` in the subject. We investigate every report.
